Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the mobile application Slip Snake (hereafter referred to as "the Application"), created and published as an arcade mobile title.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, harvest, or request any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from users. We do not require account registration, usernames, email addresses, or access to your device's contact lists.

2. Local Device Data Storage

The Application stores basic gameplay parameters locally on your individual device using sandboxed data containers. This data is strictly restricted to tracking your historical high scores, the maximum gameplay speed tiers you have achieved, and your custom mute adjustments (Sound and Vibration settings). This data remains isolated on your device and is never synchronized to external servers managed by the developer.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

To provide video revives and banner placements, the Application implements third-party services that may collect anonymous operational telemetry to deliver contextual advertisements. The Application connects to the following network layers:

  • Google AdMob: Integrated via SDK links to process banner ads, full-screen interstitial placements, and rewarded video ads. Google AdMob may utilize anonymous mobile identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to customize ad streams and assess delivery stability in compliance with standard Google privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Application does not intentionally request or retain data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal accounts or structural profile logs are engineered into our game pipelines, the Application remains inherently safe for audiences of all ages. If you are a parent or legal guardian and discover that third-party ad services have logged operational indicators from your child, please contact the respective service networks or notify us immediately.

5. Security

We value your trust in playing our game. Because all your scoring records and preferences are contained locally inside your mobile operating system's sandboxed storage directories, your baseline data privacy is safeguarded by your device's native hardware security profiles.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to align with rolling app store requirements or security adjustments. Any revisions will be marked by a new "Last Updated" timeline stamp at the top of this page.
